Instagram



Instagram is a free photo and video sharing app available on iPhone and Android.
People can upload photos or videos to our service and share them with their followers or with a select group
of friends. They can also view, comment
and like posts shared by their friends on Instagram.



The name Instagram is a portmanteau of “instant camera” and “telegram.” Systrom wanted the name to be easy
to pronounce,
spell and follow the “right here, right now” concept.
