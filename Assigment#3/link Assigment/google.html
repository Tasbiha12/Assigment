Google


Google is a popular internet search engine. It scans the Web to find Web pages that are
relevant to the words you have typed in the search box. [trademark] A search on Google for "free ring tones"
yields more than a million sites. Collins COBUILD Advanced Learner's Dictionary.

“To organise the world's information and make it widely accessible and valuable,”
