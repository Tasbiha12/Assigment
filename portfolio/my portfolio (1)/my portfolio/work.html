    First learn a little bit about your Ingredients.

    2. 700g.m Golden Rice\Basmati Rice.

    3. 3 teaspoon ginger garlic paste.
    4. 3 Large chopped tomatos.
    5. 1\2 teaspoon turmeric powder.
    6. 1 teaspoon cumin seeds.
    7. 1_ 1\2 teaspoon coriander powder.
    8. 1 teaspoon red chilli powder.
    9. 1-1\2 teaspoon biryani powder.
    10. 1 bunch of mint leaves.
    11. 1 bunch of coriander leaves.
    12. 10-12 green chillie.s
    13. 3 large onion golden fried.
    14. 1 cup curd.

    Method

      garam masala powder,coriander powder and salt.
    2. Take the chicken and marinade it in the same mixture.Let it rest for 3-4hours.
    3. Put oil in a pain,heat it and fry onions till they turn golden brown.
    4. Now, to this add the marinated chicken and cook the entire mixture for 10 mints.
    5. After that in a pressure cooker, take the rice and add 3 1\2 cups of water to it. Also take

    6. Lastly, add the cardamom powder and the chicken pieces, along with the marinade.
